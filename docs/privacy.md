# Privacy Policy for Reply AI Suggester

**Effective Date:** November 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 1, 2025

## Introduction

Reply AI Suggester ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") available on Google Play Store. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **User Input:** Text you type in messaging apps while using our keyboard service. This is used solely to generate reply suggestions and is processed locally whenever possible.
- **Personalization Data:** With your explicit consent, anonymized examples of your typing patterns and accepted suggestions for improving future suggestions.
- **Feedback:** Optional feedback you provide to help improve the service.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device model, Android version, app version, and basic usage statistics.
- **Usage Analytics:** How you interact with suggestions (e.g., acceptance rates), error logs, and performance metrics. **We do not collect the content of your messages.**
- **Network Information:** Anonymous usage patterns to optimize suggestion delivery.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Core Functionality:** Generate and display contextually relevant reply suggestions based on your typing.
- **Service Improvement:** With your consent, use anonymized data to enhance suggestion quality and accuracy.
- **Security & Abuse Prevention:** Monitor for misuse and maintain app stability.
- **Analytics:** Understand usage patterns to improve user experience (anonymized data only).

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

### Service Providers
- **AI Providers:** We use third-party AI services (Google Gemini, OpenAI) to generate suggestions. Message content sent to these services:
  - Is anonymized (no personal identifiers)
  - Is not stored by third parties
  - Uses secure, encrypted transmission
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage analytics to improve the service
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Encrypted storage for opt-in personalization data

### Legal Requirements
- We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights, users, or others.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (Primary)
- **Default Storage:** All core functionality works with data stored locally on your device
- **Encryption:** Uses Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences and Security Library
- **No Network Required:** Basic suggestions work offline

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- **Opt-In Only:** Personalization features require explicit opt-in
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted using TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at Rest:** Cloud-stored data is encrypted using AES-256
- **Access Control:** Limited access with multi-factor authentication

### Data Retention
- **Local Data:** Retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **Personalization Data:** Retained only while opted-in; can be deleted anytime
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for maximum 13 months in anonymized form

## Your Privacy Choices and Rights
